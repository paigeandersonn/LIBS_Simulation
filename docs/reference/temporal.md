# Temporal

Time evolution and detector gating: plasma-evolution models (uniform
sphere, self-similar expanding onion, or fully custom), closed-form
decay profiles for $T(t)$ and $n_e(t)$, and the gate integrator that
accumulates emission over the ICCD exposure window.
